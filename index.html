How the companion keeps kids safe

✓
COPPA-by-design. No name, age, school, or location is ever requested or stored. Nothing about your child is used to train any model.
✓
No ads, ever. The companion never markets to your child and never pushes a purchase.
✓
On-topic guardrails. The companion stays on the build and the learning. Off-topic or unsafe questions are gently redirected.
✓
Human cultural review. Community elders validate all culturally-specific content before it reaches a child.

Why this matters

Most STEM tutors treat culture as decoration. Ours treats it as the doorway in: a child sees that the wisdom in their own family's traditions is engineering, science, and math. That is what builds belonging, and belonging is what keeps kids in STEM.
